# Image Prompts for Coaching Personas

Style reference: WSJ-style editorial illustrations with whimsical, minimalist line-art characters. Simple black line drawings on white background, playful and approachable, similar to friendly robot/stick figure illustrations. Clean, editorial feel with subtle humor.

---

## 1. For Founders

**Context:** Startup founder as the de facto product leader making high-stakes decisions alone. Needs a confidential sounding board for strategy, roadmap, and investor narratives.

**Prompt:**
```
Minimalist black and white line illustration in WSJ editorial style. A lone figure standing at a crossroads with multiple directional signs pointing different ways (labeled with abstract symbols). The figure has a thought bubble with a lightbulb. In the background, a smaller supportive figure offers a compass. Simple, clean lines, whimsical and approachable style. White background.
```

**Alternative Prompt:**
```
Simple black line drawing, editorial illustration style. A founder figure standing on top of a small mountain/hill holding a telescope, looking at the horizon. Next to them, a friendly advisor character points toward a clear path among clouds. Minimalist, playful, WSJ-style. White background.
```

---

## 2. For Leaders & VPs

**Context:** Scaling leadership bench, helping rising stars master core leadership skills like giving tough feedback, managing stakeholders, and motivating teams.

**Prompt:**
```
Minimalist black and white line illustration in WSJ editorial style. A larger figure (leader) watering small plants that are growing into taller figures (team members). Some plants have small stars above them. Simple gardening metaphor for growth. Clean lines, whimsical characters, friendly and approachable. White background.
```

**Alternative Prompt:**
```
Simple black line drawing, editorial illustration style. A conductor figure with a baton leading a small orchestra of diverse stick figures, each playing different instruments. Musical notes floating above. Represents orchestrating team harmony. Minimalist, playful, WSJ-style. White background.
```

---

## 3. For ICs & New Managers

**Context:** High-performers feeling stuck, needing candid feedback and actionable frameworks to unblock career, increase visibility, and lead with confidence.

**Prompt:**
```
Minimalist black and white line illustration in WSJ editorial style. A figure climbing a ladder that transforms into stairs as they ascend. At each step, the figure grows slightly taller/more confident. A helpful character at the top offers a hand up. Simple, clean lines, whimsical and approachable. White background.
```

**Alternative Prompt:**
```
Simple black line drawing, editorial illustration style. A caterpillar-like figure on the left transforming into a butterfly figure on the right, with dotted lines showing the transformation journey. Small coaching figure with clipboard in the middle guiding the process. Minimalist, playful, WSJ-style. White background.
```

---

## Style Notes for Consistency

- **Line weight:** Medium-thick black lines, consistent throughout
- **Characters:** Simple, friendly, slightly rounded features (like the robot reference)
- **Background:** Pure white, minimal to no shading
- **Composition:** Centered, balanced, editorial feel
- **Mood:** Optimistic, supportive, professional yet approachable
- **No text:** Keep illustrations text-free for flexibility
- **Aspect ratio:** Square or 4:3 horizontal for web cards

## Color Variants (if needed)

If you want to add accent colors to match the site palette:
- **Founders:** Primary terracotta accent (hsl 14)
- **Leaders & VPs:** Secondary clay accent (hsl 30)
- **ICs & New Managers:** Sage green accent (hsl 150)
